About the size and cost of GPT-4
- Size
	- 1.8 T params / 120 layers
	- 10X bigger than GPT-3
	- Mixture of 16 experts, each w/ 111B params
	- Inference: 128 GPU cluster
- Training data
	- 13 T tokens
- Cost
	- $63 M to train
	- inference costs 3X more than Davinci (a GPT3 model?)
